About The Off-Grid Workshop

This site exists because when we started researching off-grid living, we couldn’t find a resource that was both honest and practical.

Most off-grid content falls into two categories: lifestyle blogs that show beautiful photos of cabins but avoid any real technical detail, or YouTube channels trying to sell you something. We wanted something different — a site that treats you like an adult, gives you real numbers, and tells you when something isn’t worth buying.

What We Actually Do

We test products at an off-grid homestead in real conditions. Not ideal lab conditions — real weather, real dust, real use. We’ve been building and living off-grid since 2021.

Our testing philosophy:

  • Real-world output tests, not spec-sheet numbers
  • Long-term use where possible (months, not days)
  • We use or have used every product we recommend
  • We disclose every affiliate relationship clearly

Affiliate Policy

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you buy through them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund the site and testing. We only link to products we’ve personally evaluated and would buy again.
