Our Workshop Story

What started as a personal journey to disconnect from the grid became a passion for teaching others. The OffGrid Workshop was founded in 2018 after we successfully built our own sustainable homestead in the mountains of Colorado.

We've spent years learning through trial and error, and now we're sharing that knowledge with you. Our mission is to empower individuals and families to live more sustainably, independently, and in harmony with nature.
