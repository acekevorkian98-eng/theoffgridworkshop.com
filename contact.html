Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer one-on-one consultations?

Yes! We offer limited consulting slots each month for system design, troubleshooting, and planning. Contact us for availability and rates.

Can I visit your homestead?

We occasionally host small group workshops and tours. Sign up for our newsletter to get notified about upcoming events.

Do you accept guest posts?

We're always interested in high-quality guest contributions from experienced homesteaders. Please include writing samples and proposed topics in your inquiry.

How do I support your work?

The best way to support us is by sharing our content, joining our newsletter, and engaging with our community. We don't currently accept donations but appreciate every share and recommendation!
